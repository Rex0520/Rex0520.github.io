英语语法#

句子结构#

  1. 主 + 谓: I come

  2. 主 + 谓 + 宾: I love you

  3. 主 + 谓 + 间接宾语 + 直接宾语: I gave you all my heart

    人间直宾

  4. 主 + 谓 + 宾 + 宾补: I made you happy

  5. 主 + 系 + 表: This is a beautiful night

名词#

分类#

  • 专有名词

    人或地方，机构登专有的名称

  • 普通名词

    一类人或东西或是一个抽象概念

    • 个体名词:
    • 集体名词
    • 物质名词
    • 抽象名词

名词的数#

规则#
规则
一般词尾+s
s, sh, ch, x等结尾+-es（bus->buses）
辅音字母+o结尾+es（hero->heroes）
辅音字母+y结尾，变y为i加es（study->studies）
f或fe结尾，将f/fe变为v再+es（leaf->leaves）
部分以o结尾+s（radio->radios）
复合名词一般在词尾+s，但有些复合名词将(e)s加在主体名词后面（mother-in-low->mothers-in-low、passer-by->passers-by）
不规则#

  1. 常用名词不规则变化

    child-children，foot-feet，tooth-teeth，mouse-mice，man-men，woman-women

    analysis-analyses，gooes-geese，emphasis-empases

  2. 常见单复数同型

    deer，sheep，means，species

    works，series，Chinese，Japanese，Swiss

  3. 成双成对

    • 衣物类：trousers，pants，shorts，jeans，briefs，pyjamas
    • 物品类：scissors，glasses，spectacle

  4. 群体直接为复数概念

    people，police，crew

单复数不同形#

​ goods商品，waters水域，times倍数，works工厂

​ spirits烈酒，arms武器，fishes鱼(种类)，greens青菜

不可数名词的数量表达

a cup of tea，two piece of paper，an item of news

名词的所有格#

  1. 名词的’s属格 为前置修饰，复数s结尾直接加’

    teacher’s，children’s，rabbits'

    my mom and dad’s bedroom

    my mom’s and dad’s bedroom

    the barber’s，the doctor’s，the smiths'

  2. of 属格

    a portrait of John

  3. 双重属格

    a partrait of Johns'

形容词#

形容词的语法功能#

  • 形容词做定语

    • 前置修饰语的排列顺序(直接修饰名词)

      The Chinese people have boundless creative power

    • 后置修饰词的情况

      1. 形容词被不定代词(some, any, every/body, one, thing)修饰

        Would you like something hot to drink?

      2. 某些-able和-ible结尾的形容词做定语

        This is the best means available.

      3. 表示长宽高深及年龄的形容词

        The river is about two hundred meters long and 10 meters wide

    • 形容词顺序“限观形龄色国材”

      • 限定词：a, the, an
      • 表示观点的描绘性形容词：harmful，good，beneficial
      • 表示大小，长短，高低及形状的形容词
      • 表示年龄，新旧，温度的形容词
      • 表示颜色的形容词
      • 表示国籍，地区，产地的形容词
      • 表示材料的形容词

  • 形容词做表语

    A good reputation is valuable.

    If we command our wealth, we shall be rich and free.

  • 形容词做补足语

    Knowledge makes one gentle, while association makes on perfect.

形容词比较级#

形容词 构成
单音节及少数双音节 一般-er和est
以不发音-e结尾
元音字母+辅音字母结尾，则双写辅音字母，然后加-er和-est
非重读读音的元音y结尾，将y变i再加-er和est
三音节及以上 词前加more / most
不规则形容词 good/well-better-best
bad-worse-worst
old-older-oldest：年龄的大小
old-elder-eldest：长幼关系
far-farther-farthest：距离上的远
far-further-furthest：进一步

常见的形容词的用法比较#

  • considerate体贴人的，体谅人的

    considerable相当大的（程度）

    ​ It was considerate of you not to play the piano while I was having a sleep.

    ​ It will cost considerable time to paint the furniture.

  • continunous 连续的，持续的，中间无间断的

    continual 不断的，频繁的 （强调频率）

    ​ The rain has been continuous since moring.

  • empty空的，内无一物的

    blank空白的（常指表格）

    vacant（工作）空确的

    I am looking for work, and are there any positions vacant in your firm.

  • dependable 可靠的

    dependent 依赖的

    I am still financially dependent on my parents.

    This newspaper is dependable, which always tells the truth

  • sensible明智的

    sensitive敏感的

    In the theatre, the actors are very sensitive to reaction of the audience.

    It is sensible of him to capture this golden chance.

  • sufficient 足够的

    efficient 有效率的

    They have sufficient food and clothing for their needs.

  • valuable 珍贵的

    invaluable 极其珍贵的

kids are so excity that can’t speak anything;

china is a country which is biggest than any country in asian

副词#

  • 时间副词: now, soon, then, soon, immediately, still, just, yet
  • 地点副词: abroad, above, here, there
  • 方式副词: hard, well, early, loudly
  • 频度副词: often, sometimes, ever, once, usually, twice
  • 程度副词: quite, rather, very, a lot, a little, hardly, almost
  • 疑问副词: how, where, when, why
  • 连接副词: how, where, when, whether
  • 关系副词: how, where, when, why

常见副词或副词的用法与比较#

  • right now 立刻，马上 (将来时)

    just now 刚才 (过去式)

    just 刚刚(多用现在完成时)

  • fairly & rather

    • farily修饰褒义词
    • rather修饰贬义词
    • Jim is farily clever, but Jack is rather stupid

  • already & still & yet

    • already 已经(完成时)
    • still 仍然(强调持续性)
    • yet 尚未(否定句或疑问句，完成时)

  • hard & hardly

    • hard：艰难
    • hardly：几乎不

  • also & as well & as well as

    • also 居中
    • as well 句尾
    • as well as就远原则
    • either 否定，疑问

  • high & highly

    • high 具体的高度
    • highly 抽象、程度

  • sometime & sometimes & some time & some times

    • sometime 未来的某一个时间
    • sometimes 有时
    • some time 一段时间
    • some times 几次、几倍

比较级（补充）#

as + 原级 + as：像…一样(肯定)

not so + 原级 + as：没有像…一样(否定)

（superior, inferior, senior, junior, prior）等词已经有“比较”之义

A be superior / inferior / senior / junior / prior to … B

倍数表示法#

  • 倍数 + as … as
  • 倍数 + 比较级 + than
  • 倍数 + this size / height / length / width + of

数词#

  • 年龄表示法

    • 表示某人计算的确切年龄

      • 基数词
      • 基数词 + years old
      • at the age of + 基数词
      • at + 基数词
      • 基数词 + years of age

    • 在表示某人几十多岁的约莫的年龄时，用in one’s + “十的倍数的基数词的复数”

      The hero of the story is an artist in his thirties

  • 分数表达法

    • 分子用基数词，分母用序数词
    • 当分子大于一时，分母的序数词要用复数
    • 表示几分之几的人或物时，要用介词of
    • 若做主语，谓语动词与of后的名称一致

  • 基数词+名词

    • a ten-minute walk = ten minutes' walk
    • a 50-meter-wide road
    • an eight-year-old boy (a boy of eight)

动词#

  • 谓语动词
    • 行为动词
    • 系动词
    • 情态动词
    • 助动词
  • 非谓语动词
    • 过去分词(被动 done)
    • 现在分词(主动 doing)
    • 动词不定式(将来 to do)

行为动词#

  • 行为动词（实义动词）：及物动词/不及物动词
  • 基本句型：主语 + 谓语，主语 + 谓语 + 宾语

系动词#

  • 系动词+形容词

    It felt pleasant taking a walk in the evening.

  • 系动词(become, look, make, turn) + 名词

    Her dream has now become reality.

  • 系动词 + 分词

    At last the truth became known.

情态动词#

  • can(一般现在时), be abled to, colud(过去时、完成时)

    Can you type = Are you able to type?

    We’ll be able to get you a job soon.

    He could have done the work better.

    (could have done 本能做而未做，虚拟句)

  • may; might(更加委婉)

    may / might + 完成时 对过去发生的事情的猜测

    ​ (The man might have been fired)(if he had not make an apology to his boss).(虚拟语气)

  • must, have to

    must: 必须(主观认为有必要)

    have to: 不得不(客观环境促使而不得不做)

    I must learn another language.

    I have to learn another language.

  • need

    needn’t

    ​ You needn’t do it at once, need you?

    need “需要”

    肯定: need to do

    否定: don’t need to do

    被动: need to be done / need doing

  • ought to 应该

    ought to have down (本应该做却没有做)

    You ought to have told him that the paint on that seat wet.

    Hist wife is selfish. He oughtn’t to have married her.

助动词#

  1. 帮助动词构成各种否定词或疑问句

    She didn’t come yesterday.

  2. 帮助动词构成各种时态

    She had paid her income tax.(过去完成时 had done)

    I asked her where she would spend her holidays.(过去将来时 would done)过去将来时->站在过去，展望未来

  3. 帮助动词构成被动语态 The meeting will be put off till Friday.

    Television ads are usually repeated over and over again.

  4. 帮助动词构成各种语气

    He did finish the work.(强调)

    Don’t be afraid of making mistakes.(祈使句)

    Without steel, there would be no industry.(虚拟语气)

    If you had not hurried, you would have missed the train.(虚拟语气)
